Why is this tool important?

If you're watching TV series or anime, you know the struggle: subtitles with mismatched filenames. With this tool, you no longer need to manually rename subtitles episode by episode. It renames and zips all subtitle files in a few seconds, matching them with your video files.

This tool works only on your local machine: Your files stay secure and are never uploaded to any server. It's fast, simple, and keeps your data private.

This tool is free to use! Enjoy renaming and zipping your subtitle files with ease.
